CLASSES OF FOOD

The classification of food is based on macronutrients and micronutrients. Macronutrients usually include water, fiber, fats, proteins, and carbohydrates. They are needed for our bodies in large quantities. Micronutrients include vitamins and minerals.

Carbohydrates
They provide you with energyfor daily tasks and are the primary fuel source for your brain’s high energy demands.
Protein
It has many roles in the body. It helps repair and build your body’s tissues, allows metabolic reactions to take place and coordinates bodily functions.
Minerals
It helps in keeping bones, muscles, heart, and brain working properly. Minerals are also important for making enzymes and hormones.
Vitamins
They help shore up bones, heal wounds, and bolster your immune system. They also convert food into energy, and repair cellular damage.
Fats
They provide energy for living organisms, insulate body organs, and transport fat-soluble vitamins through the blood.
Water
It Regulates body temperature, moistens tissues in the eyes, nose and mouth. It also protects body organs and tissues, and carries nutrients and oxygen to cells.
